Kim Aeri: From Idol to Actress

Kim Aeri, the lead vocalist and visual of the world-renowned group Elysian, has captivated millions with her stunning voice and elegant presence. Known as the "face of Elysian," her charm and artistry have set her apart in the global K-pop scene.

Now, Aeri takes on a new challenge: acting. With her natural charisma and ability to convey deep emotions, she has transitioned into the world of film and drama, quickly becoming one of the most promising young actresses in Korea.

Her performance at the Star Film Festival 2025 drew praise from both critics and fans, proving that Kim Aeri is more than just a musical icon—she is a multi-talented star destined to shine across industries.

As she balances music and acting, Kim Aeri continues to embody the spirit of modern artistry, redefining what it means to be a true global star.
